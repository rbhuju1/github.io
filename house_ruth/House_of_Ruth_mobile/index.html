Lucile Sims, Emma Walker, Ella R. Hampton, Jessie Taylor, and Maggie Hampton. Are all dated back to being teachers at the Fred Moore School before most schools were integrated. Once schools were integrated in 1967 the city of Denton decided there was no need for the Fred Moore school so they closed it in 1968.HOUSE OF RUTH
Civic and fraternal organizations were commonplace in Quakertown before it was razed in the 1920s. These women, pictured around 1910, belonged to the House of Ruth, an African American women’s organization that was active in Quakertown at the time. Courtesy photo/Denton County MuseumsThe women who are featured in the image are traced back to the House of Ruth. This was an African American Women’s organization active in quakertown as early as 1910.
